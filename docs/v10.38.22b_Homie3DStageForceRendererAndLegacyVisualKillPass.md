# v10.38.22b_Homie3DStageForceRendererAndLegacyVisualKillPass

## Mission

Make the procedural 3D avatar the only visible Homie lead visual.

## Why

After v10.38.22, the prototype GLB lane existed, but the visible stage still showed the older composite/anime PNG layer. This made Homie look like a mashup instead of a single 3D avatar.

## What this does

- Reinstalls a safe `HomieTrue3DAvatar.tsx`.
- Forces the 3D canvas to fill the Homie stage.
- Injects the 3D mount into:
  - `Homie.tsx`
  - `HomieBuddy.tsx`
- Removes unsafe `livingPresenceState` mood props if they returned.
- Hard-disables legacy PNG/composite/anime pseudo-image layers.
- Hides old CSS avatar internals when the 3D mount exists.
- Keeps GLB prototype asset/manifest from v10.38.22.
- Preserves routines, receipts, legacy sync, voice badges, and panel layout.

## Safety

- UI/CSS only.
- No backend rewrite.
- No voice engine internals.
- No Trading changes.
- No CardGODMode changes.
- No shell changes.
