# Homie Look and Feel Direction

## Core vision
Homie should feel like a living onboard assistant inside FairlyOdd OS, not a generic bot.

## Visual goals
- Big friendly head with readable face
- Soft rounded silhouette
- Full body presence: head, torso, arms, legs, feet
- Calm glow accents instead of harsh neon overload
- Distinct emotional states without becoming cartoon-chaos

## Motion goals
- Breathing while idle
- Subtle blinking
- Lean in while listening
- Bob slightly while speaking
- Brighter accent during celebration
- Slightly warmer accent during concern

## Personality goals
- Warm, family-safe, useful
- Energetic during wins
- Calm during blockers
- Feels like a teammate, not an alarm bell

## OS behavior goals
- Suggest next steps
- React to panel context
- Look engaged while listening or speaking
- Never feel visually dead on screen
