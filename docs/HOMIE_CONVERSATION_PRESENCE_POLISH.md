# v10.25.13 — Homie Conversation Presence Polish

This safe overlay makes Homie feel more visibly present in the UI without changing the face/look direction.

## Visible changes

- Home gets a new **Homie check-in** card at the top
- Preferences gets a new **Companion behavior** tuning card
- Homie gets a new **Companion presence** hero card and live companion tuning row
- Companion mode now has visible controls for:
  - attitude
  - proactivity
  - check-in style
  - signature line
  - proactive check-ins
  - ambient presence

## Goal

Make Homie feel less like a dashboard explainer and more like a calm, grounded companion who is in your corner.
