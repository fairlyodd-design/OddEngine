v10.35.1_FamilyHealthCalmFamilyCareLanePass

What this pass does
- turns Family Health into a calmer "what is going on / what matters first / what should we do next" lane
- adds per-member fields for:
  - symptoms now
  - symptoms started
  - refill by
  - pharmacy
  - simple support plan
- adds a household care queue
- adds an active-member action queue
- keeps the existing trusted-source research + prep-sheet flow
- keeps the same storage key: oddengine:familyHealth:v1

Files
- ui/src/panels/FamilyHealth.tsx

Install
- unzip over your OddEngine root
- overwrite when prompted
- restart the app
- open Family Health

Truth
- this pass is organizational and educational only
- it does not replace a clinician or emergency care
- urgent or severe symptoms should still go to a clinician, urgent care, or emergency services
