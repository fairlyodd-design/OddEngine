# v10.26.16a — Universal Resizable Panels + Sidebars

1. Launch OddEngine.
2. Drag the skinny handle next to the left rail to resize navigation width.
3. Open any main panel and drag the handle next to the assistant dock to resize dock width.
4. Drag the bottom handle on the assistant dock to resize dock height.
5. Drag the handle next to Mission Control rail to resize its width.
6. Drag the bottom handle on Mission Control rail to resize its height.
7. Grab any widget/card corner inside a panel and resize it.
8. Toggle panels/routes and confirm sizes stay put.
