# v10.26.20g Global Widget Drag Resize and Roomy Workspace Pass

## What changed
- widgets now have a clear drag chip
- widgets now have a visible resize corner
- floating widgets come to the front when touched
- widget z-order is remembered in the widget manager
- layout controls are roomier and easier to click

## Try this
1. Open a panel with cards.
2. Click Float on a widget.
3. Grab the little Drag / resize chip at the top-left of the widget.
4. Drag the widget anywhere on the workspace.
5. Grab the bottom-right corner handle to resize it.
6. Click Front if a widget is hiding behind another one.
7. Click Dock to send it back into the panel flow.
