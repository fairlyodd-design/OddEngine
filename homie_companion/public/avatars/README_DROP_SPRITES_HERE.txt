Drop sprite manifests and sprite sheets in this folder.

Recommended structure:
- public/avatars/your-pack/your_manifest.json
- public/avatars/your-pack/idle_sheet.png
- public/avatars/your-pack/talk_sheet.png
- public/avatars/your-pack/blink_sheet.png

Sprite manifests can be loaded directly from the Homie Companion avatar loader.
